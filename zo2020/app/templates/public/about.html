ZO-SPORTS is a website that assists you in creating, managing, and running sports tournaments. It is currently set up with templates to run athletics days, cross country, or swimming tournaments, though other tournaments are able to use the same templates, and we will be adding new sport templates.

What are we?

If you're reading this there is a pretty good chance you're one of the many people who donate a large amount of their time to ensure young people get the chance to participate in Athletics, Cross Country, Swimming and other sporting events.

Our goal at ZO-SPORTS is to do what we can to provide a site that will help people to run there large scale events. It has always impressed us the way volunteers and the others have handled the stress and pressure of these occasions, and we believe they deserve all the support we can possibly give them.

Who are we?

For several years I have worked at trying to develop a system that effectively runs these events while allowing schools and other organisations to retain the culture that has built up around the unique ways they all run their days.

In many ways this was a baling twine and number 8 wire fix. Over the last two years, with a programmer involved, we have taken the system into first a prototype desktop version, and are developing it into this website, which is far more efficient and fit for purpose

Free for NZ Schools/Clubs/Organisations... Really?

Definitely! One of our core beliefs is that schools, clubs etc have enough financial and adminitrative hassle to deal with, and we want to help out where we can. We eventually plan to fund this and further development with a sponsorship model, and all we need from you is your support and most importantly your feedback.
